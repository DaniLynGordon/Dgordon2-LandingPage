# Product Requirement Document

## 01 — Problem Statement

Dani L. Gordon is a Business Analytics & Information Systems student at the University of Iowa, graduating in May 2026, seeking full-time roles in Chicago in business analysis, marketing analytics, operations, or consulting.

The problem this product solves is the lack of a centralized space that communicates both Dani’s professional experience and her future career aspirations. While her resume and LinkedIn highlight her background, they do not fully capture her goals, interests, and direction. This landing page provides a more complete and intentional representation of who she is professionally and where she wants to go.

---

## 02 — Target Audience

The target audience includes recruiters and hiring managers at mid-size to large companies, primarily located in Chicago.

Dani is particularly interested in companies that value both data and collaboration, including organizations such as Grubhub, Kraft Heinz, Motorola, G2, Conagra Brands, McKinsey & Company, United Airlines, PepsiCo, Cisco, and Caterpillar.

The roles targeted include business analyst, marketing analyst, operations analyst, and tech consulting positions. The audience is expected to be looking for candidates who can both analyze data and communicate insights effectively.

When visitors land on the page, they should quickly understand Dani’s technical skill set, her experience working with data, and her ability to translate insights into actionable outcomes in a team-oriented environment.
